Sign up for Facebook

Facebook is a popular social network. Before you sign up, make sure you know what information they store about you and how it can be used.

Facebook will attempt to

  • Track your location Source
  • Track all your phone calls and texts (who you talk to and when) Source
  • Track much of what you do on the internet Source
  • Log most of what you do on Facebook Source

With this data, Facebook can likely

  • Guess where you live, work and spend your time
  • Guess who your friends and family are
  • Guess who you live with and meet with in real life
  • Guess your political affiliations Source
  • Guess your sexual orientation Source
  • Guess what times you work and sleep
  • Understand exactly what you're interested in
  • Estimate what your financial situation is like

You should know that

  • Facebook's founder once called people “dumb fucks” for trusting Facebook with their personal information. Source

  • Facebook makes money by giving companies, organizations and foreign powers the ability to show you ads based on what they know about you. These ads may try to change your opinion or make you buy something.

  • Facebook uses design tricks to make you give up more personal information that you may have initially intented to.

  • Facebook will run experiments on you to make you use Facebook more.
